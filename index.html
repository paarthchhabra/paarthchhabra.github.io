Paarth Chhabra is an accomplished speedcuber and a budding electronics engineer from Netaji Subhash University of Technology. What started as a mere interest in a puzzle has now become an integral part of his life, having contributed in skill development, increased duration and better quality of focus, and most importantly realising through experiences that nothing is impossible!
